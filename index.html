About Jennette McCurdy

Millions of people invite Jennette McCurdy into their homes through television, laptops, and mobile devices. Best known for her role as Sam Puckett on the hit Nickelodeon show iCarly and the new Sam & Cat, her presence and performance onscreen has captivated audiences around the world. Her talent not only lies in acting but a wide variety of other skills such as singing and dancing. But what people love most about Jennette McCurdy is her offscreen personality. She has been continually touching the hearts of all ages with her positivity, compassion and witty humor.
